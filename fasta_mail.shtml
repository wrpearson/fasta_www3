FASTA developer mailing list

If you regularly install the latest version of the fasta3 package from fasta.bioch.virginia.edu/wrpearson/fasta, you may want to join the fasta_list sympa mailing list. I plan to use this list to announce new releases and solicit bug reports.

To join the mailing list, go to:

      lists.virginia.edu/sympa/info/fasta_list

Select subscribe from the lower left of the page, and on the linked page, enter your email address, and press submit. You will be asked to confirm your intention to join the list.
